Registration Requirements

The following requirements must be met by all registrants to complete registration. Your child may not be eligible for services until all registration requirements have been met.

  • All registrants are required to pay a two-week deposit to complete registration
  • All children must have current immunization records submitted to Nyla-N-Friends
  • Any required legal documents needed to provide children services must be submitted to Nyla-N-Friends
  • A copy of all documents presented during registration may be kept on file at Nyla-N-Friends
